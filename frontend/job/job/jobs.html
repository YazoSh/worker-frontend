Produce Associate

As a Produce Associate, you get to delight customers by providing a WOW experience in our Produce Department! Some responsibilities may include:

  • Ensures a fresh and appealing produce display by keeping it stocked with quality products.
  • Provides excellent service to customers by answering general questions, providing recommendations.
  • Monitors and replenishes displays regularly to prevent spoilage or expired products
  • Maintains a clean and sanitary working and shopping environment
